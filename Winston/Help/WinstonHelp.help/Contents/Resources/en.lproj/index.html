Winston Help

A clean, modern eBook manager for macOS.

Winston keeps your eBook library organized, fetches covers and details, converts books to Kindle‑friendly formats, sends them to a connected Kindle, and collects your Kindle highlights. Pick a topic below.

Quick start

  • Add books — drag EPUB/MOBI/AZW3/PDF files onto the window, or press ⌘O.
  • Switching from calibre? — File → Import from Calibre Library… (⌘⇧O) brings your whole calibre library across with its covers, authors, series, tags and ratings.
  • See details — select a book; the inspector on the right shows its cover and metadata.
  • Send to Kindle — connect a Kindle by USB, select books, and click Send to Device.
  • Switch themes — press ⌘⇧T to cycle Purple, White, and Black.

Background work (importing, fetching metadata, converting, transferring) appears as a small status toast in the bottom‑right corner.
